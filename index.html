Your Guide to Improving Your Reading Time

Reading books is becoming a bigger and more costly challenge for many humans on planet Earth, considering how many are written and released on a daily basis. It can be difficult to know where to start.

Add on to this the ease with which a modern human can acquire and consume books, with the advent of online platforms such as Amazon, along with eBooks which can be downloaded to handheld devices at your convenience.

Learn the 4 steps to getting the most out of your limited reading time with our Ultimate Guide to Books. In it you will learn how to calculate page-to-time ratio and value based on genre, starting on the last page to see if the ending is worth reaching, and the most important skill of all: judging a book by its cover.

Readers who followed our guide saw a drastic reduction in pages read on a yearly basis by 45%.
